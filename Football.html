About Mobile

A football (or soccer) game is a team sport where two teams of 11 players try to score by getting a ball into the opponent's net, primarily using their feet.
The game consists of two 45-minute halves with a 15-minute break and is governed by rules including how goals are scored, fouls are penalized with free kicks or penalty kicks, and players can receive yellow or red cards for misconduct. The team with the most goals at the end of the match wins.
